// About Me

A bit about Simon

I'm a maker and hardware tinkerer based in Seattle. I love the process of taking an idea from napkin sketch to working prototype, designing complex CAD assemblies, routing multi-layer PCBs and fabricating with my 3D printer.

My current focus is the Takeout Box, a portable split DJ controller that I'm bringing to life through custom PCB design, touch-sensitive interfaces, and a whole lot of soldering.

When I'm not at the workbench, you'll find me on my bike exploring the Pacific Northwest, practicing my 5 ball juggling or preparing for my next DJ set.
